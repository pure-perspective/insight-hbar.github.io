Microwave Component Vendors

For established technologies such as standard HEMTs, room-temperature isolators, circulators, filters, couplers, and terminators, there are numerous large, established manufacturers in the market capable of providing a wide range of product options and large-scale production capabilities.

For specialized components required for cutting-edge applications like quantum computing‚Äîsuch as Josephson Traveling-Wave Parametric Amplifiers (J-TWPAs), cryogenic isolators/circulators operating at the mK level, specialized cryogenic filters, and advanced HEMT architectures‚Äîthe supply chain is primarily dominated by specialized startup companies.

Other requirements, such as thermalization (heat sinking), rely on system-level integrated solutions or custom designs rather than single, off-the-shelf components. Effective cryogenic thermalization of outer conductors involves a combination of specialized cables, connectors, and thermal management hardware (like thermal straps).

Advances in quantum computing depend heavily on this specialized ecosystem of component startups. For any researcher involved in building quantum hardware, the following vendors are worth considering:
